Hi, Welcome to the team

Download our E-Athlete app today and start your path to achieve your goals. E-Athlete has been developed by two high-level fencers that decided to track their training to improve their performance and achieve their short terms as well as long terms goals.

How we can help you

E-Coach

Because who knows your athlete better than you?

  • Be able to view all your athletes and their data in a simple click. Get instant feedback from their sessions to improve their training plans.
  • Keep your athletes accountable: create a path to success, monitor their progression, and understand their feelings.
  • Customise your training and objectives: you know your sport, and what needs to be done.

E-Athlete

Get a head start on the competition by introducing our systemic approach to training.

  • Our training diary is structured to give you a holistic vision of your sessions, day-to-day feelings, as well as previous and upcoming competitions. Easily reviewed by our database and graphing software, tracking made easy. Don't like our metrics? feel free to build and track your own data.
  • Keep yourself accountable by linking your profile to your team.

Who we are

Elliott Grover

Our CEO Elliott Grover is currently UK ranked no.2 and is training in Germany. An incredibly disorganised person at heart, Elliott wanted to create a training diary that it was possible for even him to stick too! So in that sense E-Athlete is the training diary for those who can’t stick to training diaries. Whether it’s as a student-athlete at Royal Holloway or a career in Finance, Elliott knows that efficiency is key and that is the solution to the problem that E-Athlete seeks to solve. Elliott’s hobbies are learning to code and listening to how much people are enjoying the product.

Philip Marsh

Having graduated in 2018 from Bristol University with a Masters degree in chemistry, I then went to Italy to train at a fencing club in Milan for two years. During this time I picked up the necessary skills to create a software company. I also realised there was a real lack of software solutions for tracking training for my sport. When Elliott came to me with the idea to make a training diary app, it seemed like a logical step for me to take.

FAQ'S

I’ve seen other Apps that serve similar purposes. What makes yours the best?

At E-Athlete you are in control. You can measure what you need to measure to improve your performance. E-Athlete approaches your sport as holistically as you do. It puts your Competition, your Diary, your Analysis and all those who you have to support you in one place.

How often are there updates to the App?

The App is regularly updated with new and interesting features on a monthly basis! Ensuring that you will be able to find the features that are right for you! Like our athletes, we are always on the lookout for ways that give us an edge over our opponents. Rest assured your performance is safe with us

It all sounds great, but what does this App actually do?

This app will be the last diary that you every buy. Data designed to be inputted within 10 minutes, making it quick and easy for the busy athlete. That you will be easily able to reflect upon and digest. We give you the tools you need to build a better athlete. What you do with it is up to you!

I'm new to ideas of Sports Performance, is this the App for me?

Everyone who has an fitness goal is an athlete. At E-Athlete we take this philosophy to heart. You'll find all the training material you need on our website as well as instructional navigation on our app to get you going and training the way you deserve to train.
